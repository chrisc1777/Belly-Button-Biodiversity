Roza specializes in bacterial species that can produce proteins that have a beefy flavor. Although many laboratories have produced meat using algae, fungi, and microorganisms found in plants, Roza hypothesizes that the best bacteria taste can be found in the human belly button. The gathered data consist of results of belly button bacteria from participants who remained anonymous for this research.
